Whisky recipes

A collection of simple cockatail recipes

Taking whisky or bourbon as your base, you can create a variety of delicious cocktails. This is intended as a guide for novices.

Essential ingredients

Keep the essentials to hand and you can combine them in a few different ways.

  • Whisky or bourbon
  • Ice
  • Sugar syrup
  • Orange bitters
  • Lemon
  • Lime

Mixers

  • Soda water
  • Tonic
  • Coke
  • Lemonade
  • Flavoured syrups

Tools

With the right tools, making cocktails is much easier but none are essential.

  • Cocktail shaker
  • Shot measurer
  • Small knife
  • Muddler
